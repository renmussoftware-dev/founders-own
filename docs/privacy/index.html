Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026
The short version. Almost everything Founders Own stores stays on your device. When you connect RevenueCat, you give us a read-only key that the app uses to read your revenue metrics directly to your phone — we don't run them through our servers, we never see your customers, and we never touch your money. We don't sell your data and we don't show ads.

This policy explains what Founders Own ("the app," "we," "us"), published by Renmus Software LLC, collects and how we use it. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Data stored on your device

Your character, quests, streaks, journal entries, and milestone progress are stored locally on your device (in the app's on-device database). This data is not transmitted to us. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

If you connect RevenueCat, your read-only API key and chosen project are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). The key is used only to read your metrics and is never sent to us.

2. Your RevenueCat revenue metrics

When you connect RevenueCat, the app uses your read-only key to fetch high-level metrics — such as monthly recurring revenue (MRR), recent revenue, active subscriptions, and active users — directly from RevenueCat to your device, to power your dashboard and verify your milestones. We do not receive, store, or process these metrics on our own servers. RevenueCat's handling of your account data is governed by RevenueCat's privacy policy.

3. Purchases and subscriptions

We use RevenueCat and your app store (Apple App Store / Google Play) to process the Founders Own subscription. When you subscribe, those services process purchase information (such as an anonymous purchase identifier and subscription status) on our behalf so we can unlock Pro features and restore purchases. To understand how the product is used, we may store a coarse, non-identifying attribute (for example, how many apps a user has connected). We do not receive your name, email, or payment details from these purchases.

4. AI features

The everyday advisor and daily quests run on your device with no external calls. Premium AI "deep-dive" features, where offered, send a summary of your own stats (such as your metrics and recent progress) to a processing service to generate the response. This summary is used only to produce your insight and is not used to build a profile of you or sold to anyone.

5. What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your data.
  • We don't show third-party advertising or use advertising trackers.
  • We don't access your RevenueCat customers, their personal data, or your payouts.

6. Your choices and deletion

  • Disconnect RevenueCat at any time to remove your stored key from your device.
  • Delete the app to remove all on-device data.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription from your App Store / Google Play account settings.
  • Email us to request deletion of any data associated with your subscription.

7. Children

Founders Own is intended for developers and businesspeople and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email renmussoftware@gmail.com.

This document is provided as a general privacy notice and is not legal advice. Please review it against your final data practices and, where appropriate, with counsel before publishing.
